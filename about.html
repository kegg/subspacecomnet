SUBSPACE COM NET is a collection of Star Trek stories that I have written over the years. These stories take place aboard the U.S.S. Fresno.

Star Trek: Fresno takes place in a universe very similar to the one that we see on the shows every week. At first when I started the series I thought about making every story take place in a different universe, but later changed that idea. If I were to do that there would be no room for improvement and growth for my characters. So I canned that idea and started a new Series Opener. I still have the two stories that I wrote in the different universes on my website, but they are just there. Those two stories don't have anything to do with the current series. The first pilot The Hatred the Burns within. ended in a way that really wouldn't allow for growth. And the second 'episode' was a story I wrote for an English Class my 12th grade year in High School titled A Man With a Heart of Oak, (which I got an A on by the way). There is a third story to the Star Trek: Fresno series titled Infiltration and a fourth titled Waiting For Death. They would have been a three part story arc. I didn't like where it was heading, it was a little too dark for me so I decided not to continue on with that project.

The new Star Trek: Fresno starts with the episode The Underground, Section 31. Now I have room for growth, my website has a new look to it for the series and I have added several other things including crew bios (which I will update as the series progresses). So my characters will grow, instead of dying after each story (which I agree would get boring after about doing it twice.)
